# Tiny Bubbles — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-07-09_

Tiny Bubbles is a calm, offline-first routine app for families. This policy
describes exactly what the app does and does not do with data. It is written to
be plain and true; it is also the source for the App Store Privacy "Nutrition
Label" and Google Play Data Safety answers.

## The short version

- **No account.** You do not sign in. There is no username, email, or password.
- **No cloud, no server.** Tiny Bubbles has no backend. Your family's data lives
  **on your device only**.
- **No analytics SDK, no ads, no third-party tracking, no data brokers.**
- **No AI.** There is no chatbot, assistant, or machine-learning model. The
  companion pet is a simple, non-AI animation.
- **We do not collect or share any personal data.**

## What is stored, and where

All of the following is stored **locally on your device** (via the operating
system's app storage) and never transmitted:

- Your child's name/display name, age mode, and companion customization.
- Routines, tasks, tokens/rewards, and completion history.
- **Optional** mood/energy check-ins (off by default; you turn them on).
- **Optional** on-device usage counts (off by default; you turn them on).
- App settings (reminders, sound/haptics, quiet hours, accessibility).

We cannot see any of this. It does not leave the device unless **you** choose to
export it (see "Your data controls" below).

## Camera and photos (optional)

Tiny Bubbles includes an **optional** "photo verify" feature a parent can enable
per task: a child may take a photo so a grown-up can confirm a chore is done.

- It is **off by default** and never required — a task is always completable
  without it, and completing a task never depends on a photo.
- Any photo is saved **only on the device** (in a temporary cache folder that is
  excluded from OS backup) and is **never uploaded, analyzed, or shared**.
- A photo is deleted when it is replaced, when you delete a child, and when you
  restore from a backup.

We disclose the camera/photo permission because the app can request it, but no
photo is ever collected or shared.

## Notifications

The app can send **local** reminders (e.g. "time for your morning routine") that
are scheduled and delivered **on your device**. No notification content is sent
to us or any third party. Reminders respect your quiet hours and are capped.

## Purchases

Any "Tiny Bubbles Plus" purchase is handled by the platform's own store
(Apple/Google) behind a parent PIN. We do not receive or store payment details.

## OS-level device backup

Tiny Bubbles turns **off** Android auto-backup of its data container
(`allowBackup:false`) and writes optional photos to a backup-excluded cache
folder, so the app's data is not swept into a cloud backup provider by the app.
On iOS, if you have iCloud Backup enabled for your device, iOS may include the
app in your **personal, encrypted device backup** — this is your own device
backup, not an upload to us. We never upload your data anywhere.

## Your data controls

- **Export / back up:** a parent (behind the PIN) can export all data to a single
  JSON file and save/share it wherever they choose. This is user-initiated and
  local.
- **Restore:** import a previously exported file to replace on-device data.
- **Delete:** a parent can wipe all child data at any time from Settings.

## Children's privacy

Tiny Bubbles is designed for families with children. Because it collects and
shares **no** personal data, uses **no** advertising or analytics SDKs, and keeps
everything on-device, it meets the stricter requirements of Apple's Kids Category
and Google Play's Designed-for-Families program. The optional child-triggered
camera is parent-enabled, off by default, never required, and never leaves the
device.
